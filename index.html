Issac Lee's mission

Issac Lee's Mission

All donations go specifically towards Issac’s mission out on the mission field. In areas where roads are non-existent, light aircraft provide the only way to reach them. Issac's training costs are covered by MAF, meaning your donations directly impact lives. This allows Issac to deliver emergency food, clothing, and Bibles to those who are entirely cut off from the rest of the world.

Why Trust MAF?

Mission Aviation Fellowship (MAF) is the world's largest humanitarian air operator. For over 80 years, they have been flying light aircraft over jungles, mountains, swamps, and deserts to deliver help, hope, and healing in more than 25 countries, supporting over 1,500 aid, development, and mission organisations worldwide.
