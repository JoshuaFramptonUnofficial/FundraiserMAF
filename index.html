Issac Lee's Mission

All donations go specifically towards Issac’s mission out on the mission field. In areas where roads are non-existent, light aircraft provide the only way to reach them. Issac's training costs are covered by MAF, meaning your donations directly impacts lives. This allows Issac to deliver emergency food, clothing, and Bibles to those who are entirely cut off from the rest of the world.

Why Trust MAF?

Mission Aviation Fellowship (MAF) is a highly respected, registered charity (Number: 1064598). For over 75 years, they have operated the world’s largest humanitarian air fleet, working with hundreds of other organisations to ensure aid gets to where it is needed most.
